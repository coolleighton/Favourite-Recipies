Roman Lentil Soup Recipe

Roman Lentil Soup is a traditional Italian dish made with lentils, vegetables, and herbs.
It's a hearty and flavorful soup, often topped with olive oil and Parmesan cheese, and perfect for chilly weather.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, choppedr
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Rinse the lentils and put them in a large pot with the water and broth.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let simmer for 20-30 minutes, until the lentils are tender.
  3. In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Add the chopped onion and garlic and saut√© until the onion is translucent.
  5. Add the cumin, coriander, and thyme, and stir to combine.
  6. Add the onion and spice mixture to the pot with the lentils.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot.
