Roman Honeyed Wine Recipe

Roman Honeyed Wine or Mulsum is a sweet wine made by mixing honey, water, and wine. The
mixture is fermented and often includes spices like cinnamon. It was a popular drink during ancient Roman banquets.

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle red wine
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 1 lemon, sliced

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the wine, honey, cinnamon stick, and cloves.
  2. Heat over low heat, stirring occasionally, until the honey is fully dissolved.
  3. Add the sliced orange and lemon to the pot.
  4. Heat the mixture for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the fruit is slightly softened and the wine is warm.
